Description







         We know that Health is Wealth. Everybody concern more about their health than anything else. But it is seen that sometimes a small disease can make a severe case due to lack of proper treatment of that particular disease. There are two reasons behind it. (1) People are very busy in today’s world. So they don’t want to go to Doctor for a disease like­ cold, cough etc. or (2) They don’t know the future problems which will be caused by these small symptoms. So if we can make such a system in which a person can check the probable disease which can be caused by the symptoms appearing in his body. In this ‘app’ data of each and every disease according to their symptoms will be stored so that a person can easily find possibility of diseases regarding to the symptoms appeared in his body. There are many diseases which have several symptoms and there are various symptoms which are the indication of many diseases. So it will be stored each and every symptoms and disease by acquiring a good knowledge in the field of medical department. One can check it in Google also. But it will be easier if an app installed in his android phone. Because, Google and other browsers depends on network and internet connection. This app will be made mainly for that specific purpose only. So it can help the people quickly to converse with an idea about his disease.
